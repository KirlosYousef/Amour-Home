Privacy Policy

Last updated: Mar 29, 2025

Amour ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Amour social media application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

By using the Service, you agree to this policy. Your name, profile details, and uploaded content are publicly visible to other users.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data

  • Account Information: Email address, first and last name
  • Public Content: Profile pictures, stories, and photos you upload
  • Subscriptions: Payment data processed by third parties (e.g., Apple/Google) – we do not store payment details

Usage Data

  • Device type, IP address, app crashes (collected only for functionality and security)

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the Service
  • To manage your account and subscriptions
  • To troubleshoot technical issues
  • We do NOT:
    • Sell your data to third parties
    • Use tracking cookies or analytics
    • Share your data for advertising

3. Data Sharing

  • Public Content: Your name, photos, and stories are visible to all users
  • Service Providers: Only with trusted partners (e.g., hosting companies) under strict confidentiality
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law (e.g., court orders)

4. Data Retention & Deletion

  • We retain your data only while your account is active
  • Delete your account via Settings to remove public content (backups may persist for 30 days)

5. Security

  • We use encryption and secure servers
  • Note: Publicly shared content can be copied by others – share responsibly

6. Children’s Privacy

  • Users under 13 are prohibited
  • Users aged 13-17 must have parental consent

7. Changes to This Policy

We’ll notify you of material changes via email or in-app notice 30 days in advance.
